Transport / Trafnidiaeth

It would be a fools-errand to argue that Wales is well-connected. Whether it's the 4 hours 30 minutes Express service from Cardiff to Holyhead, or the fact that more Welsh people fly from Bristol than Cardiff, Transport for Wales certainly has its work cut out. But if you pride scenery over connectivity, then Wales is the place to be, whether that's a heritage railway or the A470.
